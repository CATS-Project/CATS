Purpose of the project

CATS (Collection and Analysis of Tweets made Simple) is an open source web interface developed by researchers from the universities of Lyon and Bucharest, and its purpose is to enable everyone to generate a corpus of tweets with specific demands (keywords, geolocation, language \textit{etc}), even people without any programing background. Not only does CATS allow people to collect tweets, it also enables one to analyze them thanks to various tools developed in the field of machine learning (event detection, named-entities recognition, topic modeling, word clouds visualization, etc...). CATS relies on a robust implementation that can scale up to massive data streams.
