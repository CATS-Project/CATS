Natural language processing

Vocabulary analysis

The vocabulary is the list of all lemmas sorted in terms of their frequency in tweets.




Term co-occurrence exploration

A set of terms are said to be co-occurring when are present in the same tweet. Co-occurence analysis can help understand semantics or dicover linguistic patterns for instance.





Named-entity recognition

Named-entity recognition (NER) consists in automatically locating names of persons, organizations and locations in tweets.



Topic modeling

Topic modeling is a way of automatically discovering hidden themes that pervade a collection of tweets. Topic models can help organizing, understanding, and summarizing large amounts of tweets.

LDA

LDA (i.e. Latent Dirichlet Allocation) is a model that represents tweets as mixtures of topics, each topic being associated to a probability distribution over a set of words.







Hierarchical LDA

Hierarchical LDA (hLDA) is an extension of LDA where topics are joined together in a hierarchy.

MABED

MABED (i.e. Mention-Anomaly-Based Event Detection) is a statistical method for automatically detecting significant events reported in a collection of tweets.
